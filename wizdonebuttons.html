📖 Game Information

📜 How to Play

Welcome to Arcane Wizard Duel! A turn-based wizard battle game where you fight against various elemental wizards.

Basic Gameplay:

  • Select an enemy wizard from the "Fight Wizard" menu
  • In battle, choose spells to cast against your opponent
  • Manage your mana — each spell costs mana
  • Use positioning — different ranges affect spell effectiveness
  • Defeat enemies to gain XP, gold, and loot
  • Level up to increase stats and unlock new abilities

✨ Spells

🔗 Elemental System & Combos

Advantages: Fire > Ice (+50%), Ice > Storm (+50%), Lightning > Water (+50%)
Using an element against its weakness deals -25% damage
🔥 Fire + Fire: Longer burn | ❄️ Ice + Ice: Stronger freeze | ⚡ Lightning + Lightning: Chance to stun
📍 Positioning: Close (+20% physical, -20% spell) | Medium (balanced) | Far (-20% physical, +20% spell)

⚠️ Status Effects

🔥 Burn — 2 dmg/turn for 3 turns
❄️ Freeze — May skip turns for 2 turns
⚡ Shock — Drains 5 mana/turn for 2 turns
☠️ Poison — 3 dmg/turn for 3 turns
